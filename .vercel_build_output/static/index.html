Strengthening OUR Community

This project, funded by the Bushfire Community Recovery Resilience Fund, is a response to the community’s feedback following the 2019 bushfires.

As a community we share the same environment.  We want our community to feel prepared for future challenges and be armed with knowledge that helps us to tackle whatever natural disaster comes our way.

Above all, we want our community to feel prepared, safe and connected.
